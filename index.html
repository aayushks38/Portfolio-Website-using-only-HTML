8. Projects

CTC Decoder

CTC Decoder is a modern, responsive web application that helps students, fresh graduate, and job seekers estimate their actual take-home(in -hand) salary instead of relying only on advertised CTC(Cost to Company). The platform allows users to input their annual CTC, bonus amount, Provident Fund(PF) deduction percentage, and income tax percentage. Based on these values, the application performs real-time calculations to display the final take-home salary.
